Signal classification

The purpose of signal classification is to build a model that learn from labeled data from clustering step. then use that model to predict/classify all signals in the whole data set.

The labeled data from clustering step has more than 20 classes. That means, the problem is multiclass classification. There are many algorithms, that serve well in multiclass classification such as Support Vector Machine (SVM), Decision Tree, Naive Bayes, Random Forest.

Each algorithm was applied on our data set. And then the results were analyzed and compared
